Contact Lens Cutting Tools

Diamond tools are used by contact lens manufacturers for manufacturing Contact lens.

Special purpose tools are also developed as per customer drawing & specification.

We meet or exceed the requirements of any current contact lens manufacturer. Different materials demand different tool geometries to maximize cutting efficiency. Based on a standard arc of 120 degrees (other angles are available. All tools are wear-resistant and designed to maintain their precision for a maximum length of time. Quality control is set to keep the edge chip free at 500x magnification while maintaining a smooth and facet-free radius. Standard tools are frequently used with two-axis lathes for roughing-out purposes. We developed a variety of tools using a conical or cylindrical profile to meet the growing demand for two-axis machining systems. Used in direct lathing of contact lenses, our tools may be ordered with either natural or synthetic diamonds, set into standard solid shanks or into the more versatile insert system.

Note : - We also provide customized tools as per customers requirment.
